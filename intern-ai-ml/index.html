AI / Machine Learning Intern

Part-Time / Learning · Certificate Only · Final Year & Freshers

Internship Program — This is a certificate-only, part-time / learning internship. No salary is provided. You gain hands-on experience and an official Blueidealteck internship certificate.

AI / Machine Learning Intern Internship

Work on real AI and machine learning projects at Blueidealteck. You will build predictive models, develop AI automation scripts, and contribute to client-facing AI solutions. This internship is perfect for students passionate about data science, NLP, or computer vision.

What You'll Work On

  • Build and train ML models using Python, TensorFlow, or PyTorch
  • Work on data cleaning, preprocessing, and feature engineering pipelines
  • Integrate AI models into web applications via REST APIs
  • Research and implement AI techniques for client use cases
  • Document model performance and prepare progress reports

Who Should Apply

  • Final-year B.E./M.E./MCA in CS, Data Science, or related field
  • Basic knowledge of Python and libraries like NumPy, Pandas, Scikit-learn
  • Understanding of ML concepts: classification, regression, clustering
  • Familiarity with Jupyter Notebook or Google Colab
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving mindset

What You Get

  • Official Certificate: Blueidealteck internship completion certificate recognised on LinkedIn and resumes.
  • Real Project Experience: Contribute to live client projects — not dummy tasks.
  • Mentorship: Guided by senior engineers and project leads throughout the internship.
  • Flexible Hours: Part-time commitment — ideal for students still in college.
  • LinkedIn Recommendation: Top performers receive a LinkedIn recommendation letter.
