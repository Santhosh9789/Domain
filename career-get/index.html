Remote | Performance-Based Contract

Graduate Engineer Trainee (Freshers)

Batch: 2022 - 2025 Graduates

Career-a start panna nalla platform thedureengala? Blueidealteck offers a "Learn & Earn" model where you work on real-world software projects. No fixed salary, but unlimited learning and project-based incentives.

Who Can Apply?

  • Education: BE/B.Tech (CS/IT/ECE), BCA, MCA, or any degree with a passion for coding.
  • Pass-out Year: 2022, 2023, 2024 graduates and 2025 final year students.
  • Skills: Basic knowledge of HTML, CSS, JavaScript. Understanding of any one backend language (Python/Node.js/PHP) is a plus.

What You Will Do (Responsibilities)

  • Build & Deploy: Assistance in developing web applications and custom software modules.
  • Debug & Fix: Identifying and fixing bugs in existing projects to ensure smooth performance.
  • Fast Learning: Attending internal training sessions and quickly implementing new tech stacks.
  • Documentation: Maintaining clear documentation for the code you write.

The Blueidealteck Performance Model (Compensation)

  • Project Incentives: Intha role-ku fixed monthly salary kidaiyaathu. Neenga contribute panra ovvoru project milestone-kum ungaluku Project Fee/Incentive kidaikum.
  • Experience Certificate: Successful-a project mudika mudika, ungaluku valuable professional experience certificate valangapadum.
  • Growth Path: Based on your performance, neenga "Junior Developer" role-ku fast-track-la promote aagalam with higher commission rates.

Ideal Candidate Profile

  • Self-starter who doesn't need constant supervision.
  • Ready to work in a bootstrapping startup environment.
  • Strong fundamentals in Coding & Problem Solving.
