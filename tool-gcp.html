Why GCP?

Google Cloud Platform (GCP) is a suite of cloud computing services that runs on the same infrastructure that Google uses internally for its end-user products, such as Google Search, Gmail, and YouTube. It excels in data analytics and machine learning.

  • Big Data: Unmatched capabilities in big data processing with tools like BigQuery and Dataflow.
  • AI & ML: Access to cutting-edge AI models and tools like Vertex AI.
  • Network: Runs on Google's private, software-defined network, one of the fastest in the world.
