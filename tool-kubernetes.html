Why Kubernetes?

Kubernetes (K8s) automates the deployment, scaling, and management of containerized applications. It has become the operating system for the cloud, managing clusters of hosts to run efficient and resilient workloads.

  • Automated Rollouts & Rollbacks: Progressively rollout changes to your application, monitoring health to ensure zero downtime.
  • Service Discovery & Load Balancing: Easily expose containers using DNS or their own IP addresses.
  • Self-Healing: Restarts containers that fail, replaces and reschedules containers when nodes die.
