UI/UX Designer

Freelance | 1-4 Years Experience

Craft Intuitive & Stunning Experiences

We are seeking a creative UI/UX Designer to work with us on a project-by-project basis. You will be responsible for designing user-centric interfaces for web and mobile applications.

Key Responsibilities

  • User Interface Design: Create visually appealing designs, mockups, and prototypes using Figma or Adobe XD.
  • User Experience: Conduct user research and create wireframes to ensure intuitive navigation and flow.
  • Collaboration: Work closely with developers to ensure designs are implemented accurately.
  • Branding: Maintain brand consistency across all digital touchpoints.

Requirements

  • Experience: 1-4 years proven experience in UI/UX design.
  • Portfolio: Strong portfolio showcasing web and mobile design projects.
  • Tools: Proficiency in Figma, Adobe XD, Photoshop, Illustrator.
  • Skills: Knowledge of HTML/CSS is a plus.

Compensation

  • Payment Model: Per Design / Per Project Basis.
  • Flexibility: Work on your own schedule with clear deadlines.
  • Remote Work: 100% remote.
