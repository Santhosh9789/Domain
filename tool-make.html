Make

Visual platform to automate tasks, workflows, and apps.

Why Make?

Formerly Integromat, Make lets you design, build, and automate anything from simple tasks to complex workflows. Its visual interface allows for complex logic, branching, and error handling.

  • Visual Designer: Drag and drop apps and modules to create scenarios that look like flowcharts.
  • Advanced Logic: Built-in support for routers, filters, iterators, and aggregators to handle complex data structures.
  • Flexibility: Connect to any API, even if there isn't a pre-built app for it.
