Why Adobe XD?

Adobe XD is a powerful and easy-to-use vector-based tool for designing and prototyping user experiences for web, mobile, and voice. It's part of the Creative Cloud ecosystem, ensuring seamless integration with Photoshop and Illustrator.

  • Vector Design: Create crisp, scalable designs for any screen or device.
  • Voice Prototyping: Go beyond the screen and design voice interactions and playback.
  • Auto-Animate: Create smooth transitions and micro-interactions by simply moving objects across artboards.
