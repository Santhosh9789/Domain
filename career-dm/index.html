Digital Marketing & Growth Lead

Remote | 3-6 Years Experience

Drive Our Brand & User Acquisition

We are seeking a data-driven Digital Marketing Lead to spearhead our growth strategies. You will be responsible for defining and executing marketing campaigns that drive brand awareness, lead generation, and customer acquisition.

Key Responsibilities

  • Strategy & Execution: Develop and implement comprehensive digital marketing strategies including SEO/SEM, email marketing, and social media.
  • Lead Generation: Drive high-quality B2B leads through targeted campaigns (LinkedIn, Google Ads, Content Marketing).
  • Analytics & Reporting: Monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), analyze campaign performance, and optimize for better ROI.
  • Brand Management: Oversee content creation and manage the company's online presence to ensure brand consistency.

Requirements

  • Experience: 3+ years in digital marketing, preferably in B2B Tech/SaaS.
  • Skills: Deep understanding of SEO/SEM, Google Analytics, LinkedIn Ads, and CRM tools.
  • Content: Strong copywriting skills for creating compelling ad copy and landing pages.
  • Growth Hacking: Proven track record of scaling user acquisition with creative low-cost strategies.

Compensation

  • Performance-Based: High commission on qualified leads and closed deals generated through your campaigns.
  • Remote Work: Fully remote role.
  • Bonus: Quarterly performance bonuses based on growth targets.
