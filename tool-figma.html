Figma

The collaborative interface design tool.

Why Figma?

Figma connects everyone in the design process so teams can deliver better products, faster. It's built for the web, so you can design, prototype, and gather feedback all in one place.

  • Real-time Collaboration: Design together in real files in real-time. No more versioning issues.
  • Prototyping: Create interactions and animations to show how your design feels, not just how it looks.
  • Design Systems: Build libraries of reusable assets to ensure consistency across projects.
