The world's most comprehensive and broadly adopted cloud platform.

Why AWS?

Amazon Web Services (AWS) offers over 200 fully featured services from data centers globally. It provides the infrastructure to power everything from small startups to the largest enterprises and government agencies.

  • Broad Service Offering: From basic compute (EC2) to advanced AI (SageMaker) and Serverless (Lambda).
  • Scalability: Elastic infrastructure that grows and shrinks with your demand.
  • Global Reach: Deploy your application to multiple regions around the world with a few clicks.
