Social Media Intern

Remote Internship | Fresher (2023-25)

Kickstart Your Digital Marketing Career

Join us as a Social Media Intern and learn the ropes of digital branding. You will assist in managing our social media channels and engaging with our audience.

Key Responsibilities

  • Content Scheduling: Assist in scheduling posts across LinkedIn, Instagram, and Twitter.
  • Engagement: Reply to comments and engage with relevant communities to boost visibility.
  • Analytics: Track post performance and assist in creating monthly reports.
  • Creativity: Brainstorm ideas for engaging social media campaigns.

Requirements

  • Eligibility: Freshers (2023-2025 pass-outs) or current students.
  • Interest: Passion for social media trends and digital marketing.
  • Skills: Basic knowledge of Canva or design tools is a plus. Good communication skills.

Compensation

  • Structure: Monthly Stipend + Performance Lead Bonus.
  • Certificate: Internship completion certificate provided.
  • Job Offer: Opportunity for full-time employment based on performance.
