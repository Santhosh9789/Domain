Senior Full Stack Developer

Remote | 3-6 Years Experience

Lead Our Engineering, Architect Scalable Solutions

We are looking for an experienced Full Stack Developer to lead the architecture and development of high-performance web applications. You will work on complex problems, lead junior developers, and drive technical excellence.

Key Responsibilities

  • System Architecture: Design and implement scalable, secure, and high-performance applications using the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express, React, Node.js) or Python frameworks.
  • Full Lifecycle Development: Manage the complete software development lifecycle, from requirement analysis and design to testing, deployment, and maintenance.
  • Code Quality & Mentorship: Enforce best practices, conduct code reviews, and mentor junior developers to ensure high-quality code delivery.
  • Optimization: Troubleshoot performance bottlenecks and optimize applications for maximum speed and scalability.

Technical Requirements

  • Experience: 3-6 years of hands-on experience in full-stack development.
  • Frontend: Expertise in React.js, Next.js, Redux, HTML5, CSS3/SCSS.
  • Backend: Proficiency in Node.js, Express.js, or Python (Django/FastAPI).
  • Database: Strong knowledge of MongoDB, PostgreSQL, or SQL Server.
  • DevOps: Familiarity with AWS/Azure, Docker, and CI/CD pipelines is a plus.

Compensation & Benefits

  • Competitive Pay: Performance-based high commission/contract fees per project milestone.
  • Remote Work: 100% remote working culture with flexible hours.
  • Growth: Opportunity to transition into a Tech Lead or CTO role based on performance.
